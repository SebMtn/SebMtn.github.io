I am rich

October 23, 2023

I am rich, so rich.
I don’t like to brag, but here’s the detail of my net worth:

  • time with my kids: 3 hours/day
  • relationship with my wife: 19 years
  • playing sports: 10 hours/week
  • work on meaningful, self-selected projects: 8 hours/day
  • live in a beautiful country of my choice: 365 days/year

Do you want to know the secret of how I became rich?
I quit my job as a trader.
Because I realized that job only made a number on a bank’s server grow.

And you know who I met in my previous jobs?
I met people who were nice and smart. But I also met the poorest people I know.

They had no time with their family. They were stressed every day. They had been divorced several times. The only thing they had in their life was seeing a big number on their banking app.

When I realized that the best scenario for me, what everyone called success, was to end up poor like them, I decided to quit.

I quit my job, left my home, left London. I moved to Portugal, a country with a much lower cost of living, and where the culture emphasises personal relationships rather than personal wealth. I started to work on Ethical Capitalism. An idea dear to my heart, with 0 income prospects, that I felt would make a true difference in the world.

I have to confess I was very afraid to take the plunge.

Am I a good father if I don’t pay for London private schools to my kids? Am I husband-material if I don’t earn a one-percenter income? Am I even worth talking to if I don’t have a fancy job?

And underlying all this: How can this massive life change be right, if no one I know is doing this?

But I eventually realized all my fears were outward-facing. Turns out, the harshest judge was in my head. Once I managed to turn him down a notch, I started to live. And be rich.
